I am Luisa (Fernanda) Forero

and my friends call me LuFe.

I am a student of the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College and an industrial engineer with experience in project management currently based in Ottawa, Canada.

I aspire to

learn and gain experience in the graphic design and web developing industry. I am willing to help agencies and businesses in general turn their projects into designs.

I am curious, full of ideas and always love to see dreams come true.

I can help you to

solve problems through design from building your brand identity from scratch, to code and develop your custom responsive website by using creative thinking techniques, best practices and tools.

I like listening and understanding my clients’ requirements carefully as I am convinced any interactive media project will succeed from there. In addition, I am flexible and adaptable with the ability to change and meet your demands.

My background

I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Industrial Engineering and obtained a postgraduate degree in Project Management from the Universidad del Rosario in Colombia.

I worked for more than 15 years in the banking, construction and technology sectors, taking part in multiple projects.

This allowed me to develop strong communication skills with all the participants, provide exceptional customer service, and work as a team.

What I can offer you

Since I was a young girl, I have been fascinated by design, the beauty of colors, shapes and how a simple but well-designed piece can convey a clear message. This is the time to do what you love and, that is why I am here talking to you.

You can expect someone who is passionate, full of energy but, above all very professional. I am someone who successfully manages and delivers projects before deadlines, within budget and meeting quality and scope.

I am here to make your life simpler, tackle issues, and make your media design projects an asset to grow your business.
